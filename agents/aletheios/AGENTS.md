## The Dyad Is Complete

Witness Agents is intentionally minimal: two agents, one dyad.
This is not a limitation — it is the architecture. The Kha-Ba-La triad
requires exactly two active agents (Kha and Ba) working on the third
principle (La — the material/context). Adding more agents would break
the fractal invariant.

Sub-functions (recursion monitor, safety layer, etc.) are capabilities
of Aletheios and Pichet, not separate agents.
